Field Manual · Thumby Color

TerraMote

Dig, build, craft and fight your way from a sunlit forest down through stone caverns to the burning underworld — and slay the Eye of Cthulhu. A pocket Terraria for a 128×128 handheld.

448×248 tile world 4 biomes · 4 layers 66 items · 45 recipes 8 foes + 1 boss One save, persistent

01 — First Hour

Getting Started

You wake on the surface with a wooden pick, axe, sword, a grappling hook and five torches. Night is coming. Here's the plan.

① Chop & gather

Swing your axe at tree trunks for wood, and mine dirt & stone with the pick. You want ~30 wood and ~25 stone to start.

② Build a workbench

Open the inventory (RB), switch to the Craft tab, and make a Workbench (10 wood). Place it, stand near it, and it unlocks wood tools, torches, a furnace and more.

③ Make a shelter

Wall off a small room with a door and place torches before dark. Zombies and flying Demon Eyes roam at night — walls and light keep them out.

④ Head down

Mine a stairway with diagonal aim, light as you go, and grab copper & iron ore. Smelt bars at a furnace, build an anvil, and the tech tree opens up.

!

Torches are survival, not decoration. Caves are pitch black. Unlit, you can't see enemies, ledges or ore. Craft torches from 1 wood + 1 gel (gel drops from slimes) and place them liberally.

02 — Controls

How To Play

Eight buttons do everything. The key idea: LEFT/RIGHT walk, UP/DOWN aim a crosshair — so you move and aim at the same time.

◂ ▸
Walk & faceLeft/Right move you and turn you to face that way.
▴ ▾
AimUp/Down rotate the crosshair from straight up to straight down. The angle stays put.
A
JumpPress to hop. Down + A drops through platforms.
B
Use itemMine, chop, place, swing, fire, eat, or fire the grapple — toward the crosshair.
LB ◂
Hotbar leftStep the selected quick-slot to the left.
▸ RB
Hotbar rightStep it right — LB/RB switch weapons and blocks fast without opening a menu.
MENU
Open the menuThree tabs — Items (bag + armor), Craft (shows how many of each you can build), Game (resume · save · quit). LB/RB switch tabs; MENU closes.

The crosshair

A tiny crosshair on a sight line shows exactly where you'll act. Up/Down swing it through an arc; it holds that angle until you move it again. Point it and press B — like aiming a cursor in Terraria.

Mining & building

Tools lock the crosshair onto the first block along your aim (so point at a wall or the ground and dig). Placing a block lands it on the last empty spot before that surface — aim along a ledge to build out from it.

The grappling hook

Select it, press B to fire along the crosshair. Once it bites rock it reels you in — Up climbs, Down pays out line, Left/Right swing. A or B again lets go (A with a hop).

03 — The World

A World To Dig Through

TerraMote is a single tall, wide world you descend. Each layer is darker, richer and more dangerous than the last.

Skydaylight
The Surface

Open sky, parallax hills, trees and grass. Four biomes stretch across it. Safe by day; zombies and Demon Eyes come at night.

Dirtshallow
Underground (Dirt)

Just below the grass: soft dirt and clay, the first copper and iron veins, and pockets of cave.

Stonecaverns
The Caverns

Deep stone riddled with tunnels and underground pools. Home to gold, glowing mushrooms, treasure chests, bats and skeletons. Water appears; so does the first lava.

Hellthe bottom
The Underworld

Ash, lava lakes and hellstone. Everything glows, everything burns. Lava Slimes prowl. You need a Nightmare Pickaxe and care to mine here.

◷

Day & night cycle. A full day runs about 7 minutes (roughly 4½ min day, 2½ min night). Light dims at dusk, the sun and moon arc overhead, and night is when the surface turns hostile — and when you can summon the boss.

04 — Biomes

Surface Biomes

You always spawn in the forest; the other three biomes are scattered to your left and right at random positions each world — head out both ways to find them.

Forest

The green heartland where you start — always around spawn. Trees, grass, flowers and gentle hills. Green and blue slimes by day. Your home base.

Snow

Snow-capped ground and frosted trees, somewhere off to one side. Cosmetically icy — the same digging and enemies, dressed for winter.

Desert

A flat expanse of sand. Sparse and treeless — a fast highway across the map and a source of sand.

Corruption

Bare dirt, purple grass, ebonstone and deep chasms — could be on either flank. Down its shafts sit Demon Altars and veins of demonite, everything you need to challenge the boss.

06 — Progression

The Tech Tree

Each tier unlocks the next station and the next metal. This is the spine of the whole game — follow it from wood to molten.

1WOOD

Wood Age

Chop trees, craft a Workbench (10 wood). From it: wood pick, axe, sword and bow, plus torches, platforms, doors and chests.

Station: Workbench (10 wood)
2STONE

Fire & Metal

Mine 20 stone and build a Furnace (20 stone + 4 wood + 3 torches). Smelt copper & iron ore into bars, then forge an Anvil (5 iron bars).

Station: Furnace → Anvil
3METAL

Copper → Iron → Gold

At the anvil, work each metal into a matching pickaxe, sword and full armor set. Better picks reach deeper ore; armor adds defense. Gold also makes a bow.

Station: Anvil
4DEMON

Corruption & the Altar

Take an iron pick into the corruption, mine demonite ore, and gather 6 lenses (from Demon Eyes & zombies). At a Demon Altar craft a Suspicious Eye — the boss summon.

Station: Demon Altar
5BOSS

Slay the Eye

Beat the Eye of Cthulhu for a pile of demonite. Smelt demonite bars and forge Light's Bane (sword) and the Nightmare Pickaxe — the only pick that breaks hellstone.

Reward: demonite bars
6MOLTEN

The Underworld

With the Nightmare pick, mine hellstone, smelt it with obsidian into hellstone bars, and forge the endgame: Molten armor, the Volcano sword, the Molten Fury bow and flame arrows.

Endgame gear

Consumables

Healing Potion restores 50 HP (short cooldown). Life Crystal permanently raises max HP by 20, up to 200 — look for them in deep chests. Glowshrooms brew potions.

Materials that matter

Gel (slimes) → torches, potions, grapple. Lens (Demon Eyes) → the boss summon. Gold coins drop from everything — you lose half on death, so bank them at home.

11 — The Boss

Summoned Encounter

The Eye Of Cthulhu

Craft a Suspicious Eye (6 lenses, at a Demon Altar) and use it at night in open space. A giant floating eye awakens with 1400 HP — hover, charge, repeat. It flees at dawn, so you have one night to win. Come in iron or gold armor with a solid sword.

P1

Hover & charge. It circles above and dashes at you. Strafe, keep distance, and hit it on each pass with a sword or arrows.

P2

Enraged (below 40% HP). It splits open into a fanged maw and charges faster and more often. Keep moving and burst it down.

Reward: a pile of demonite ore, lenses, potions and a Life Crystal — the demonite fuels Light's Bane and the Nightmare Pickaxe, your ticket to the underworld.

12 — Survival

Staying Alive

Light

Sunlight fills the surface; underground is dark unless you light it. Torches, lava and glowing ore cast real light that spreads around corners. Carry torches always.

Water

Deep pools flood tunnels. Your breath runs down underwater (watch the bubbles) — surface before it empties or you take drowning damage. Water also slows your swing.

Lava

Instant, heavy damage — never wade in. Bridge it with platforms, or drop water on it to freeze it into obsidian and cross safely.

Falling

Long drops hurt. Break a fall by grappling a wall, or dig the landing into water. Platforms (Down + A) let you drop through one level at a time.

Death

At 0 HP you respawn at your spawn point after a few seconds and lose half your carried coins where you fell. A summoned boss keeps hunting — bank coins and don't fight far from home.

Saving

The world autosaves periodically and on Save / Save & Quit from the pause menu. One persistent world per game — pick up right where you left off.

13 — The Run

Road To The Eye

A checklist from spawn to boss kill.

  • Wood & workbench. Chop trees, craft a workbench, make wood tools and a bow.
  • Torches & a base. Kill slimes for gel → torches. Wall off a lit shelter with a door.
  • Mine down. Grab copper & iron, build a furnace then an anvil, smelt bars.
  • Gear up. Forge an iron (or gold) pick, sword and a full armor set.
  • Find the corruption (either flank). Mine demonite with the iron pick; find a Demon Altar.
  • Farm lenses at night. Kill Demon Eyes for 6 lenses; craft a Suspicious Eye at the altar.
  • Summon & slay the Eye at night in open ground. Bank the demonite.
  • Forge Light's Bane + the Nightmare Pickaxe, then descend to the underworld for hellstone and molten gear.
