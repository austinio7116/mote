Twenty games for the Thumby Color, built on the Mote engine. Pick a game to see it on the device. Install and update straight from the gallery — in Mote Studio, or right on the handheld (press RB in the Mote launcher with a device docked) — or grab any game's .mote and drop it in /mote/ by hand. Games marked 2P play multiplayer — USB cable, LAN, or internet rooms, set up right on the device.
