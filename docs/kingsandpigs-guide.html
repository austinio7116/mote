🏰 The Raid

The pigs have taken the castle. You are the King — bearded, furious, and armed with a hammer far too big for a throne room. Descend through the keep floor by floor, smashing every pig between you and the exit door, and drop to the next level. Go as deep as you dare; the run ends when your three hearts run out.

No two raids are the same. Every floor is carved fresh — a winding stone descent stitched together from 69 hand-authored rooms: pillared halls, staircases, arch galleries, treasure vaults and cannon nests, all wrapped in the pack's real brickwork with sunlight pouring through the arched windows.

The hammer is your everything: it kills pigs, smashes crates, and — crucially — bats things back. Time a swing at a thrown box, a lit bomb or a cannonball and it flies the other way, back into whatever threw it.

🐷 The Pigs

The garrison is a menagerie of green pigs, each with its own trick. Learn them — most can be turned against each other.

Pig

The rank-and-file guard. Patrols its ledge and charges when it spots you. One clean hammer swing puts it down — but they rarely come alone.

Box Pig — thrower

Lobs wooden boxes across the room. Swing at an incoming box to send it straight back and knock the thrower flat.

Bomb Pig — thrower

Hurls lit bombs. Bat one back for a bigger bang — or just get clear before the fuse burns down. Whacked bombs bounce off walls, so mind the ricochet.

Box Ambusher — hidden

Sits disguised as a harmless crate until you wander close, then pops the lid and lunges. Smash suspicious boxes from a safe distance.

Cannon Gunner — from Floor 2

Crews the wall-mounted cannons, striking a match to fire a heavy iron ball down the hall. Swing at the ball to return it straight into the gunner who lit it — the sweetest kill in the keep.

The King Pig — boss

Guards the exit on every third floor and will not stray far from it. He takes eight hits and hits hard — bait his lunge, punish the recovery, and swing back anything he throws your way.

💎 The Descent

Loot

  • Diamonds — the score. Sweep every chamber; the deeper vaults are richer.
  • Hearts — restore a life. Rare, so don't take needless hits.
  • Crates — smash for a chance at loot… or an ambusher.

Getting around

  • Plank bridges lie across every floor drop — walk over them, press ▼ to fall through, or jump back up through them.
  • Arched windows light the rooms but are just scenery — the real routes are the doors, drops and staircases.
  • Lost? LB pulls up the floor map; the door out is at the bottom.

Tip: when a floor gets crowded, retreat to a doorway and let the pigs funnel to you — then bat their own bombs and boxes back down the line.
